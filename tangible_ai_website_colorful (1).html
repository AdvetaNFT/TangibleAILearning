The gap

Most AI tools give answers on screens. Children need experiences.

Young learners need touch, movement, conversation, repetition, imagination and adult connection. TangibleAI turns AI into a guide for hands-on learning instead of another app for the child to consume.

What the app creates

🧱

Maths with objects

Counting, addition, patterns, measurement and fractions using blocks, beads, LEGO and shells.

🌿

Science with nature

Observation walks, plant life, weather, magnets, water, shadows and simple experiments.

📚

Language with stories

Reading prompts, vocabulary, narration, sequencing, oral storytelling and reflection.
