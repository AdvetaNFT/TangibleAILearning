The Problem

Most AI learning tools today are built around more screen time, faster answers, and child-facing digital interfaces. For young children, this can reduce hands-on exploration, movement, focus, imagination, and adult-child interaction.

Children need more than answers.

They need touch, rhythm, repetition, curiosity, storytelling, outdoor life, and meaningful thinking.

Our Solution

Adult-Controlled AI

Parents and teachers choose the year, subject, topic, materials, and learning goal.

Screen-Free Activities

AI creates activities using blocks, books, clay, LEGO, maps, leaves, shells, and movement.

Child-Safe Design

No open chat for children. No addictive rewards. No random answers. No scrolling.

How It Works

Select Learning Goal

Choose Year 1–8, subject, topic, duration, and available materials.

Generate Activity

AI creates a short, hands-on lesson with adult script and open-ended questions.

Observe Progress

The adult records focus, understanding, creativity, language, and next steps.
